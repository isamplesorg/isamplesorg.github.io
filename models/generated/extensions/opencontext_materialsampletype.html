1 Artifact

The concept Artifact
with URI https://w3id.org/isample/vocabulary/specimentype/1.0/artifact
is defined in vocabulary https://w3id.org/isample/vocabulary/specimentype/1.0/specimentypevocabulary

This is the top concept of the vocabulary.

Immediately narrower concepts:

Architectural element, Clothing, Coin, Container object, Domestic item, Ornament, Photograph, Pot sherd, Tile, Utility item, Weapon

Definition:

An object made (manufactured, shaped, modified) by a human being, or precursor hominid. Include a set of pieces belonging originally to a single object and treated as a single specimen.

2 Organism part

The concept Organism part
with URI https://w3id.org/isample/vocabulary/specimentype/1.0/organismpart
is defined in vocabulary https://w3id.org/isample/vocabulary/specimentype/1.0/specimentypevocabulary

This is the top concept of the vocabulary.

Immediately narrower concepts:

Bone object

Definition:

Part of an organism, e.g. a tissue sample, plant leaf, flower, bird feather. Include internal parts not composed of organic material (e.g. teeth, bone), and hard body parts that are not shed (hoof, horn, tusk, claw). Hair is tricky, include here for now. Does not necessarily imply existance of parent sample. Not fossilized; generally includes organism parts native to deposits of Holocene to Recent age.

Path from the top concept:
Organism part->Bone object

Definition:

Sample is an individual bone or part of a bone from an animal.
